Fonts and Typography

Headings:

For headings, the typography font Roboto is being used as it is a neat and simple font without too much variation thus giving the page a clean feeling while still being able to present itself as a header without too much distractions.

Paragraph:

For paragraphs, the typography font Montserrat is being used as it is pretty similar to Roboto with a small variation. The font is also reminicent of the typography being used in local newspapers so as to give a sense of nostalgia and belonging towards the page and a feeling of "this news is important" when it comes to the articles
